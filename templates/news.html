Inaugural General Meeting

2012-02-27

The Jamalaide Inaugural General Meeting will be held in the Clubs Assiciation common room at 1800 on the 13th of March. We need at least 10 Adelaide University students present to officially form the club. Please come along and have a say in how we move forward.

Pirate Kart Jam Next Weekend

2012-02-18

Jamalaide are hosting a Pirate Kart Jam over the weekend of February 25th-26th. All of the games will then be submitted to the Pirate Kart that is being shown off at the Game Developers Conference! Check the link for details and registration.

Global Game Jam This Weekend

2012-01-24

The 2012 Global Game Jam runs from 5pm this Friday through to Sunday evening. Come along to the University of Adelaide Clubs Association and make a game in 48 hours!

New Look and GGJ Registrations Open

2011-12-04

We are chuffed-to-weenies about our new site design. Even chuffier than that though, is the fact that we have been confirmed as running a site for the Global Game Jam! To join the fun you must register on their site and on ours. Terribly inconvenient.

LD22 and GGJ

2011-11-23

Ludum Dare 22 inbound, let's get a posse. On the 15th of December the droogs will meet in the Hub to jam it down. I have been informed that there will be no posse or droogs, but I'm going to be in the Hub at 1200 anyway. On a more sombre note, we are also hoping to run a Global Game Jam site in late January and are currently in discussion with the organisers.

Website Existential Crisis Resolved

2011-11-23

Shiny! Check out our sexy new digs with purple trim and extra topping. We'll be riding our art mule hard to bring you some succulent retro graphics which will probably be too rubbish to use, but keep your F5 key hot anyway!

Adelaide Game Jam Over

2011-11-20

After thirty two hours the participants stumbled out of the Clubs Association, clutching their hard-earned tim-tams and shambling off to find a place to rest. Big and/or biggest props to Colin for showering us in Adelaide Game Jam decals from his sticker shoppe. We were really surprised by the quality of the entries; some of them were actually playable!
